Research Assistant

As a research assistant, I collaborated with a professor to develop a psychological study in Unity, aimed at testing cognitive processes in a virtual reality environment. My responsibilities included designing and implementing interactive features for the VR environment to simulate experimental conditions, optimizing the usability and functionality of the system, and ensuring the environment met the study's requirements. Additionally, I assisted in troubleshooting technical challenges, testing the VR setup, and refining the design to enhance participant experience and study reliability. All code was writeen in C# using unity.
